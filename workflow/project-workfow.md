# Project Workflow

Every Scotty Digital project follows the same high-level process. The details may vary, but the goal is to reduce cognitive load, improve consistency, and create better experiences for both clients and visitors.

---

## 1. Discover the Story

Before discussing layouts, colors, or technology, understand the client's story.

Ask:
- Who are they?
- What motivates them?
- What should visitors feel in the first 10 seconds?
- What action should visitors take?

---

## 2. Design the Experience

Establish:

- Information Architecture
- User journeys
- Content hierarchy
- Key interactions

Structure before style.

---

## 3. Build the Design Language

Only after the experience is defined:

- Photography
- Typography
- Color
- Spacing
- Components

---

## 4. Build for Two Users

Every website has two audiences.

### Visitors

The public experience.

### Editors

The client experience.

The CMS should match the client's desired level of independence.

Ask:

"How often will the client update this?"

"If they want to update it themselves, can they learn it in five minutes?"
