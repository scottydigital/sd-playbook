# Storytelling Through Design

Design is communication.

Every meaningful design decision should support the client's story.

## Design with Purpose

Every major layout decision should have a narrative reason.

Don't ask:

"Does this look cool?"

Ask:

"What does this communicate?"

## Example: Sally Zito

Hero concept:

- 30% Family
- 40% Compassion
- 30% Music

The center image is intentionally larger because compassion is the value that connects every part of Sally's life.

The layout itself communicates her story before the visitor reads a single word.
